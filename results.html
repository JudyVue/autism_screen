Low Risk

Total score is between 0 - 2

If your child is younger than 24 months, screen again after the child's second birthday.

No further action required unless surveillance indicates risk for ASD.

Below, please find additional resources in your area.

Medium Risk

Total score is between 3 - 7

Administer the Follow-Up (the second stage of the M-CHAT-R/F) to get additional information about at-risk responses.

If M-CHAT-R/F score remains at 2 or higher, the child has screened positive. Action required: refer child for diagnostic evaluation and eligibility evaluation for early intervention.

If the child's score on Follow-Up is 0-1, the child has screened negative. No further action required unless surveillance indicates risk for ASD.

Child should be rescreened at future well-child visits.

Below, please find additional resources in your area.

High Risk

Total score is between 8 - 20

It is acceptable to bypass the Follow-Up and refer immediately for diagnostic evaluation and eligibility evaluation for early intervention.
